Learning Solution

We develop learning solutions for different stream of Education such as K12, Higher & Technical Education

Learning Solution is developed through scientific enquiry; inferential learning; solving problems through design; cross relating knowledge of different domains of science, and strengthening knowledge of a wide spectrum of sciences or engineering disciplines to understand, investigate and solve complex problems related to real world scenarios.

Considering the global education we have associated ourselves with vastly experienced and eminent Subject Matter Experts, who are thorough and proficient with state-specific curriculum requirements. These experts correlate content development process with region-specific curriculum, accurately, to fill the gaps in the existing curriculum.

  • Learning Solution Approach

Learning solution is developed for Theory subjects of School, Higher Education and Technical Education

The solution is end-to-end content driven does not provide any on-line lecturing/Tutioning

It will supplement the learning process in Classroom or self learning

It is mapped with Syllabus of respective University/State Boards/Autonomous Institutions

Lecture/Learning Content is provided with Text, images, Sketches and 3D/2D demonstrations

2D/3D demonstrations are embedded across learning content

2D/3D demonstrations are provided with voice-over in English and other regional languages

Tutorial Problems are explained with help of real life

Provide unique methodology in developing the e-Content in regional languages

Language conversions are done using Artificial Intelligence Programs and validated by Subject Matter and Language experts, manually for correctness

Learning solutions are delivered as per learning technology standards such as SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model), TIN, CAN, W3C & Other required standards.
